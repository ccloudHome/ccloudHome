Assignment: Phishing Awareness Poster:

Your task for this assignment is to create an informative poster about phishing awareness. Your poster should include the following elements:

1. Definition of phishing and explanation of how it works.

2. Examples of common phishing tactics, such as email spoofing and deceptive links.

3. Tips for identifying phishing attempts, such as checking sender email addresses and avoiding clicking on suspicious links.

4. Consequences of falling victim to phishing attacks, such as financial loss and identity theft.

5. Recommendations for protecting against phishing attacks, such as enabling email filters and reporting suspicious emails.

Be creative with your design and use visuals to enhance the impact of your poster. You can hand-draw your poster or use digital tools for design. Make sure your poster is clear, concise, and visually appealing.
