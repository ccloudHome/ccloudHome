Assignment: Phishing Email Analysis

For this assignment, you will analyze a phishing email and identify key indicators of its fraudulent nature. Follow these steps:

1. Obtain a phishing email from a reliable source, such as a cybersecurity training platform or your instructor.

2. Examine the email carefully and identify elements that raise suspicion, such as unfamiliar sender addresses, spelling or grammar errors, or urgent language.

3. Evaluate any links or attachments included in the email. Do not click on them; instead, analyze the URL structure and file names for signs of deception.

4. Write a brief analysis of the phishing email, highlighting the specific indicators of its fraudulent nature and explaining how you would respond to it to avoid falling victim.
